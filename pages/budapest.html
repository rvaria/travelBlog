Budapest

Budapest had a range of spectacular locations, most of which we were able to visit whilst I was there. One of the first places we went to was Buda Castle. It took a good couple of hours to go around the place fully but it was filled with spectacular sights and a deep history.

From Buda Castle, there was a great view of the city, although unfortunately, it happened to be quite cloudy specifically whilst I was at the castle. Later on in the day the weather warmed up a little and we made our way to Gellert Hill Waterfall. Climbing to the top of it gave another view of the city from a different angle.
